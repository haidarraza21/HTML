Suresh Gyan Vihar University

Courses: B.Tech, BCA, Deploma, Agri.
Suresh Gyan Vihar University is a non-profit autonomous research university located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India.
In 2017, the university became the first private university in Rajasthan to be awarded an "A" grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council.
The University was established through the Suresh Gyan Vihar University, Jaipur Act (Act no. 16 of 2008) of the Government of Rajasthan.
